 Exposure Risk Events The Exposure Risk Events widget displays a list of exposure-related risk events that you can review and take steps to remediate. Action Description Investigate a risk event Click the name of a risk event to view it in Threat and Exposure Management. From there, you can view more details and take remediation steps.
