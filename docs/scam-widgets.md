 Scam widgets Understand scam detections in your environment. Scams often involve deceptive emails designed to trick recipients into providing sensitive information or transferring money. Widget Description Scam Detections Displays the total number of scam detections for each for each protected cloud application or service. Top 5 Scam Email Senders Displays the top 5 senders that sent the most scam email messages. Top 5 Scam Email Recipients Displays the top 5 recipients that received the most scam email messages.
