 1.2.17 - Ensure that the --audit-log-maxage argument is set to 30 or as appropriate (Automated) Profile applicability: Level 1 - Master Node Retain the logs for at least 30 days or as appropriate. Retaining logs for at least 30 days ensures that you can go back in time and investigate or correlate any events. Set your audit log retention period to 30 days or as per your business requirements. Note By default, auditing is not enabled. Audit Run the following command on the Control Plane node: ps -ef | grep kube-apiserver Verify that the --audit-log-maxage argument is set to 30 or as appropriate.
