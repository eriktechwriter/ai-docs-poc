 Policy Status The Policy Status tab displays all the products installed on the target endpoint, any Trend Micro Apex Central policies assigned, and the current policy status for each installed product. Note Trend Micro Apex Central identifies and associates users with endpoints based on the type of endpoint or through integration with Active Directory.
