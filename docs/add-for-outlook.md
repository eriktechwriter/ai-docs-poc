 Add-in for Outlook The Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection Add-in for Outlook provides an easy way to involve your users in protecting their Exchange Online mailboxes. The add-in allows users to manage their emails quarantined by Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection in Outlook and report emails to Trend Micro as false positives and false negatives to help improve threat detection. Note End users can manage the emails quarantined by both API-based protection and Inline Protection for Exchange Online.
