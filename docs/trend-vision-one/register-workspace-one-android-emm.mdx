# Registering Workspace ONE as your Android EMM provider

Register VMware Workspace ONE as your Enterprise Mobility Management (EMM) provider with Google.

:::warning[Important]

The following Workspace ONE UEM instructions and screen captures were valid as of July, 2022. For further help, check your Workspace ONE UEM documentation.
:::

### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  Sign into the Workspace ONE UEM console, and select any level of organization group (OG) from the hierarchy structure.

2.  Access the **Android EMM Registration** screen and begin to register with Google.

    - If you selected the "Customer" type OG:

      1.  On the **Getting Started** tab, select **Workspace ONE**.

      2.  Under **Android EMM Registration** in the **DEVICE ENROLLMENT** section, click **CONFIGURE**.

          <figure>
          <img src="./images/WorkspaceONE_Configure_img=04618b6d-087f-493d-90f4-07e13807fee1.webp" />
          </figure>

      3.  On the **Android EMM Registration** screen, click **REGISTER WITH GOOGLE**.

          <figure>
          <img src="./images/WorkspaceONE_Register_with_Google_img=529736b1-8755-4c8a-97e6-6b42e819a300.webp" />
          </figure>

    - If you selected a non-"Customer" type OG:

      1.  Go to **GROUPS & SETTINGS → All Settings → Devices & Users → Android → Android EMM Registration**.

          <figure>
          <img src="./images/WorkspaceONE_Register_with_Google_img2=d823cc8c-d5bb-422c-b83f-5d179f8d854e.webp" />
          </figure>

      2.  Click **REGISTER WITH GOOGLE**.

3.  Sign in with a Google Account and click **Get started**.

4.  Type your business name and click **Next**.

    <figure>
    <img src="./images/WorkspaceONE_Business_Name_img=b7307dc1-e871-4e01-b0a5-9a46d5c45a32.webp" />
    </figure>

5.  (Optional) Type your contact details.

    <figure>
    <img src="./images/WorkspaceONE_Contact_Details_img=659ff36d-d244-4b79-a9b9-b64a1c64f62b.webp" />
    </figure>

6.  Accept the Managed Google Play agreement and click **Confirm**.

7.  Click **Complete Registration**.

Workspace ONE is registered as your Android EMM provider and you are redirected back to the Workspace ONE UEM console.
