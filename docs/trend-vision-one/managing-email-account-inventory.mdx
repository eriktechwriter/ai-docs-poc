# Managing the email account inventory

Perform actions and set policies for email accounts in the Email Asset Inventory app.

When accessing the Email Asset Inventory app, you can click on any of the **Available Actions** or items in the **Email Asset Inventory** section of the left panel to display a list of associated email accounts. From this view, you can perform several actions.

<td><p>View email account details</p></td>
<td><p>Click on an email account to open the <strong>Detailed Profile</strong> screen</p>
<p>The <strong>Detailed Profile</strong> screen consists of two tabs.</p>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>Basic</strong>: Provides basic information about the email account such as the associated email address, managing protection solution, and if email sensor detection is enabled for the email account.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Policy</strong>: Provides an overview of enabled policies. Click <strong>Manage Policy</strong> to configure your policy settings.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Manage policies</p></td>
<td><p>Select one or more email accounts and click <strong>Manage Policy</strong> to configure policy settings</p>

<ul>
<li><p>For managing policies and key features with Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection, see <a href="trend-vision-one-manage-policies-cecp">Managing email account policies with Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection</a>.</p></li>

<li><p>For enabling key features with Cloud App Security, see <a href="trend-vision-one-enable-features-email-cas">Enabling key features for email accounts with Cloud App Security</a>.</p></li>
