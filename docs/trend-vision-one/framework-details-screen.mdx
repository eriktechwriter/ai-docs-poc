# Framework details screen

Learn more about compliance summary and analysis results for the selected framework or standard.

The following table outlines the actions available in the **Framework details** tab.

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 50%" />
<col style="width: 50%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><p>Action</p></th>
<th><p>Description</p></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><p>Check your organization's security configurations against your selected frameworks and standards</p></td>
<td><p>Click <strong>Check configurations</strong> to perform a manual check.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Select frameworks and standards</p></td>
<td><p>Click <strong>Frameworks and standards</strong> to select the compliance frameworks and standards that your organization uses.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>View asset group pass rates for your endpoint assets</p></td>
<td><p>Click <strong>View asset group pass rates</strong> to display the pass rates for your tagged endpoints.</p>
<p>For more information, see <a href="trend-vision-one-compliance-asset-groups-tags">Create asset groups and assign asset tags</a>.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>View your pass rate for each control family in the selected framework</p></td>
<td><p>Click any pass or fail number to view more details.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Create CSV reports of your compliance analysis for selected frameworks in the Reports app</p></td>
<td><p>Click <strong>Manage reports</strong> and select a framework or standard to generate a CSV report.</p></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
