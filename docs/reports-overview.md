 Reports Overview Trend Micro Apex Central allows you to generate, download, and send reports that consolidate data from all registered managed products without having to log on to multiple product consoles. You can use Trend Micro Apex Central to: Generate one-time reports on demand. Add scheduled reports to automatically generate and send reports to specified recipients on a user-defined schedule. Create custom report templates from data views or use pre-defined custom templates and static templates.
