Steps to modify Cloud Risk Management Scan frequency Input a value for Delay between runs. Click Update Scan settings. Once modified, the interval will be displayed on the Settings page. {.zoom} Note Cloud Risk Management Scan does not allow you to set up a frequency of less than an hour.
