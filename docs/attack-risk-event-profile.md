 Attack risk event profile View details about attack-related risk events to better understand possible impacts and available remediation actions. To change the status of risk events, click Manage risk event to go to the Threat and Exposure Management app. The following table details the tabs in the attack risk event profile screen. Tab Description Basic Displays general information about the risk event, as well as mitigation options, preventative measures, and informational references. Impacted assets Lists the assets in your organization impacted by the risk event, as well as the latest risk score, account type, job title, data source, and date of detection. Related alerts Lists all Workbench alerts related to the risk event, as well as the case management status, alert score, associated incident, and time of creation.
