Trend Vision One Mobile

Trend Vision One Mobile is a complement to Trend Vision One that lets you:

  • See a summary of your critical and high-severity Workbench alerts in the last 7 days.

  • Share information about your Workbench alerts with your team members.

  • Get notifications and alerts from the Trend Vision One console such as Response Task notifications and unusual product status alerts.

The Trend Vision One mobile app is available in the Google Play Store and the App Store (iPhone & iPad).
