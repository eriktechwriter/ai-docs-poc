# Trend Vision One cases

Open cases in Trend Vision One and manage your organization’s cases in **Workflow and Automation → Case Management**.

Case Management displays Trend Vision One cases opened by your security operations center (SOC) team, information technology (IT) team, or risk manager. Trend Vision One cases are based on incidents, events, and alerts in Cyber Risk Exposure Management, Security Playbooks (using the Automated Response Playbook), Workbench, and Compliance Management. Case Management automatically closes Trend Vision One [cases that are inactive for 60 days](case-management-faq.mdx).
