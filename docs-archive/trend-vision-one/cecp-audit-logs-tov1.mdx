# Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection audit logs available in Trend Vision One

May 23, 2025—Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection sends its audit logs to Trend Vision One. Administrators can filter and view these logs alongside other Trend Vision One audit logs in a centralized location - the Audit Logs screen under Administration.

**Administration → Audit Logs**
:::
