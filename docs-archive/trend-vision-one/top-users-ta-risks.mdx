<td><p>Top 5 High-Risk Email Recipients</p></td>
<td><p>Displays the top 5 internal users that received the most emails with high-risk threats including phishing, malicious files, ransomware, and malicious URLs over the selected time period. These users are most targeted by serious attacks and may require your immediate attention.</p>
<p>This widget allows you to easily know the high-risk email receiving trends and the total high-risk threats distributed by the top 5 users. Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection collects statistics for this widget from all protected email service types, including Exchange Online and Gmail.</p>
<p>Click the three dots next to each user name, and then click <strong>Create Training Campaign</strong> to create a Security Awareness Training training campaign to educate the user about common attack methods used by cybercriminals.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Top 5 Spam and Graymail Recipients</p></td>
<td><p>Displays the top 5 internal users that received the most spam and graymail messages over the selected time period.</p>
<p>This widget allows you to easily know the spam and graymail receiving trends and total spam and graymail received by the top 5 users. Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection collects statistics for this widget from all protected email service types, including Exchange Online and Gmail.</p>
<p>Click the three dots next to each user name, and then click <strong>Create Training Campaign</strong> to create a Security Awareness Training training campaign to educate the user about common attack methods used by cybercriminals.</p></td>
