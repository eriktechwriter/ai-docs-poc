# Accessing the management console

### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  Open the management console logon page.

2.  Do the following based on the user account you use:

    ::: {.simpletable-container}
    <table id="GUID-B2B5D176-FB7B-4B68-B9B5-8309E03468D8__choicetable_iyh_3pb_g1b" class="choicetable" data-border="1">
    <colgroup>
    <col style="width: 50%" />
    <col style="width: 50%" />
    </colgroup>
    <thead>
    <tr class="chhead table-header-style">
    <th class="choptionhd">Option</th>
    <th class="chdeschd">Description</th>
    </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
    <tr class="chrow">
    <td class="choption"><p>Customer License Portal (CLP) or Licensing Management Platform (LMP) root account</p></td>
    <td class="chdesc"><ol>
    <li><p>Type the user name, and then click somewhere outside of the user name text box.</p>
    <p>The management console redirects you to the corresponding CLP or LMP logon page for Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection.</p></li>
    <li><p>Type the user name and password, and then click <strong>Sign In</strong>.</p>


    :::note

    <p>If your account is enabled with two-factor authentication, you still need a verification code generated by the Google Authenticator app to sign in, beside your account credentials.</p>
    <p>Two-factor authentication provides an added layer of security for your Trend Micro account and prevents unauthorized access to your Trend Micro SaaS product consoles, even if your password is stolen.</p>


    :::

    </li>
    </ol></td>
    </tr>
    <tr class="chrow">
    <td class="choption"><p>Local account (Active Directory account)</p></td>
    <td class="chdesc"><ol>
    <li><p>Type the email address, and then click somewhere outside of the user name text box.</p>
    <p>The management console redirects you to your Microsoft Entra ID or your organization's Active Directory Federation Services (AD FS) sign-in page.</p></li>
    <li><p>Type the password for your AD account, and then click <strong>Sign In</strong>.</p></li>
    </ol></td>
    </tr>
    <tr class="chrow">
    <td class="choption"><p>Local account (non Active Directory account)</p></td>
    <td class="chdesc"><p>Type the email address and password, and then click <strong>Log On</strong>.</p></td>
    </tr>
    </tbody>
    </table>
    :::
