# Managing Email Sensor detection

Enable or disable Trend Vision One email sensor detection and response on supported email accounts.

After granting the necessary access permissions, you can choose to modify the email accounts that report to Trend Vision One at any time.

### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  Go to **Email and Collaboration Security â†’ Email and Collaboration Sensor**, and click **Sensor Management**.

2.  Go to the **Exchange Online**, **Gmail**, or **Other Email Services** tab.

3.  Click **Manage Sensor Detection**.

4.  In the **Available Users/Groups** list, select the accounts or groups you want to monitor.

5.  Click the arrow (![](/images/simulationsRightArrow=20220525102311.webp)) to move the selected accounts to the **Selected Users/Groups** list.

6.  (Optional) Click the delete icon (![](/images/xmark_icon=773fb77a-7552-4201-85f7-8d8bfb8f3251.webp)) to remove an account or group from the **Selected Users/Groups** list.

    :::warning[Important]

    After you disable email sensor detection, email accounts stop reporting activity data to Trend Vision One.
    :::

7.  Click **Save**.

    Trend Vision One starts monitoring activity data for the selected mailboxes.
:::
