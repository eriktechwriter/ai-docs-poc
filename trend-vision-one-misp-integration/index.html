MISP integration (via Service Gateway)

Trend Vision One enables transfer of suspicious object data and retrieval of threat intelligence data from the MISP threat sharing platform through a Service Gateway.

note

To enable the direct transfer of suspicious object data and retrieval of MISP threat intelligence data, see MISP integration (via direct connection).
