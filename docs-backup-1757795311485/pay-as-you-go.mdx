# Pay-as-you-go

View information about your pay-as-you-go contract and all Trend Vision One features supporting pay-as-you-go billing on the **Pay-as-you-go** tab.

Pay-as-you-go billing offers a consumption-based option for accessing supported Trend Vision One features, and eliminates the need to estimate usage in advance and allocate credits.

For more information, see [Introduction to pay-as-you-go](introducing-payg.mdx).

The **Summary** section of the **Pay-as-you-go** tab provides details on your pay-as-you-go contract and AWS account.

- If you have not yet purchased a pay-as-you-go contract, click **Purchase a contract in AWS** to [buy a pay-as-you-go contract from AWS Marketplace](purchasing-payg-contract-aws.mdx).

- If you already have a contract, you can see your **AWS account ID** and go to the [AWS Billing and Cost Management console](https://console.aws.amazon.com/costmanagement) to view your usage and billing details.

The **Pay-as-you-go features** section displays any supported Trend Vision One features currently using pay-as-you-go. Click **Go to app** next to a pay-as-you-go feature to review your usage in the related app.

For the pricing information of pay-as-you-go features, see [Pay-as-you-go pricing information for Trend Vision One features](payg-pricing-info.mdx).

For more details on how pay-as-you go works, see [Frequently asked questions](payg-faqs-section.mdx).
