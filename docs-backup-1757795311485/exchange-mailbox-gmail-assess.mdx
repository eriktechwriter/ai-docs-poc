# Exchange Online Mailbox/Gmail Assessment

Scan all messages sent and received in the last 15 to 30 days for all cloud mailbox users in your environment .

You can always find your previous assessment reports by clicking **Assessment History** on the **Cyber Risk Assessment** screen.

### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  In the Trend Vision One console, go to **Assessment → Cyber Risk Assessment**.

2.  In the **Exchange Online Mailbox/Gmail Assessment** area, choose **Office 365** or **Google Workspace** from **Start Assessment**.

3.  Run the mailbox assessment.

    <td><p>Office 365 mailboxes</p></td>
    <td><ol>
    <li><p>On the <strong>Office 365 Mailboxes</strong> screen, choose to <strong>Scan all mailboxes in your organization</strong> or <strong>Scan your mailbox</strong> and click <strong>Grant Permission</strong>.</p></li>
    <li><p>Grant <strong>Cyber Risk Assessment</strong> the permission to access your mailbox(es) for scanning by clicking <strong>Accept</strong> on the Office 365 permissions page.</p></li>
    <li><p>Confirm the scope and start the assessment.</p>
    <p>Depending on the number of messages, the assessment may take between a few minutes and a few hours to complete. The service will send the results to your mailbox for review.</p></li>
    </ol></td>

    <tr>
    <td><p>Google Workspace mailboxes</p></td>
    <td><ol>
    <li><p>On the <strong>Google Workspace Mailboxes</strong> screen, click <strong>Install App &gt;</strong> and install the assessment app in your organization's domain.</p>
    <p>To install the Google Workspace Marketplace app, you must sign in to your administrator account and temporarily grant <strong>Cyber Risk Assessment</strong> the permission to access your domain data.</p></li>
    <li><p>Sign in to the app with your administrator credentials.</p></li>
    <li><p>Confirm the scope and start the assessment.</p>
    <p>Depending on the number of messages, the assessment may take between a few minutes and a few hours to complete. The service will send the results to your mailbox for review.</p></li>
    </ol></td>
    </tr>
    </tbody>
    </table>

4.  Wait until all your mailbox(es) finish the assessment.

    You will receive an email notification when the assessment is complete. Click the link in the notification to access the Trend Vision One console, and click **View Report** in the **Exchange Online Mailbox/Gmail Assessment** area.

5.  Check the results on the assessment report and take risk remediation actions immediately if necessary.
