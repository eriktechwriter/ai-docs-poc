# Cyber Risk Exposure Management data sources

View and manage the Trend and third-party data sources that supply data to **Cyber Risk Exposure Management**.

To enable **Cyber Risk Exposure Management** capabilities, including asset discovery, vulnerability assessment, and risk event detection, you must have one or more Trend Micro or third-party data sources connected to Trend Vision One. The **Cyber Risk Exposure Management** section in **Data usage and monitoring** under **Data Source and Log Management** displays supported data sources along with the connection status and data target. For details on all supported Cyber Risk Exposure Management data sources, see [Configure Cyber Risk Exposure Management data sources](configuring-data-sources.mdx).
