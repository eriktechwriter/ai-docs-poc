# Get started with training campaigns

Create a training campaign to educate your users on cyber threats.

:::warning[Important]

Training campaigns are only available after you [upgrade your pricing package](credit-req-for-apps-services.mdx) to Cyber Risk Exposure Management - Essentials.
:::

### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  On the Trend Vision One console, go to **Cyber Risk Exposure Management → Security Awareness**.

2.  Click **Create Training Campaign**.

3.  Configure your campaign and select a training program.

    1.  In the **Campaign name** field, enter a name for your campaign.

    2.  Choose a training program for the campaign.

        1.  Click **Select Training Program**.

        2.  Select the training program you want to use for the campaign. There are currently over 100 video training programs and over 150 interactive training programs available in the following languages:

            You can select as many training programs as your organization needs to create a comprehensive training campaign.

        3.  Click **Save**.

    3.  Click **Next**.

4.  Select training participants.

    1.  In the **Participant List** section, select the users to participate in the training.

    2.  Click **Add to Training Campaign**.

    3.  When you are done adding participants, click **Next**. You may add up to 200 participants.

5.  Set up invitations for the training campaign.

    1.  In the **End time** field, set the time and date for the training campaign to conclude.

    2.  To preview the invitation message for participants, click **Preview**.

        :::tip

        To customize the content of the invitation message, click **Edit** in the preview.
        :::

    3.  Click **Next**.

6.  Set up reminders for the training campaign.

    1.  To remind participants to complete the training before the end date, enable reminders.

        1.  In the **Send Reminders** section, set **Status** to enabled.

        2.  To preview the reminder message for participants, click **Preview**.

            :::tip

            To customize the content of the reminder, click **Edit** in the preview.
            :::

        3.  To schedule more than one reminder, click add (+) to add and configure additional reminders.

    2.  To notify participants after the training is completed, enable the completion notification.

        1.  In the **Send Completion Notification** section, set **Status** to enabled.

        2.  To preview the completion notification for participants, click **Preview**.

            :::tip

            To customize the content of the notification, click **Edit** in the preview.
            :::

7.  After you have configured the training campaign, click **Finish**.
