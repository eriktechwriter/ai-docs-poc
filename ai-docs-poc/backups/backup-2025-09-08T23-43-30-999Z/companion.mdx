:::: {role="main"}
Analyze, investigate, and respond to incidents and alerts using the power of AI.

Trend Companion is an AI-powered cybersecurity chatbot that helps you to investigate, analyze, and respond to Workbench alerts, generate **XDR Data Explorer** queries, and answer any cybersecurity questions.

To use Trend Companion, your system administrator must [opt in to all pre-release apps/services](platform-directory.mdx).

:::warning[Important]

- Each user can send up to 100 messages per day and 10 messages per minute. The message limit resets every day at 00:00 UTC.

- Prompts have a 500-character limit.

- Trend Companion has [filters to remove personal information](frequently-asked-questions.mdx) from prompts.
:::
