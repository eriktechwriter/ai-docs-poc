### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  Log into your AWS console.

2.  Go to **AWS Marketplace → Manage subscriptions → Trend Vision One - Zero Trust Secure Access**.

3.  Under **Actions**, select **Launch CloudFormation Stack**.

4.  Select:

    1.  **Fulfillment option**: Private Access connectors with high availability

    2.  **Software version**: the latest Zero Trust Secure Access - Private Access version

    3.  **Region**: the appropriate region

5.  Click **Continue to Launch**.

6.  Click **Launch** to begin creating the CloudFormation Stack.

7.  Modify the template:

    1.  Open the **Infrastructure Composer**.

    2.  Open the **LaunchTemplate** component to display its Details.` `Under **Resource Configuration**, scroll to **MetadataOptions**.

    3.  Change HttpTokens to “required" and click **Save**.

8.  Click **Create template**.

9.  Continue following the [instructions](private-access-aws-marketplace.mdx) for deploying the CloudFormation stack as per AWS CloudFormation stack instructions. The registration token used can be obtained in Trend Vision One under the Amazon Web Services Platform.
