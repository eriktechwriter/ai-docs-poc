Collect and manage digital evidence to support threat investigation and incident response.

[Forensics](forensics.mdx) **Evidence Archive** allows you to collect and manage evidence packages from the endpoints in your environment.

The following table outlines the actions available for **Evidence Archive**.

<colgroup>
<col style="width: 25%" />
<col style="width: 75%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><p>Action</p></th>
<th><p>Description</p></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><p><a href="trend-vision-one-evidence-collection">Collect evidence</a></p></td>
<td><p>Click <strong>Collect Evidence</strong> to gather evidence from the endpoints in your environment.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Filter endpoints</p></td>
<td><p>Use the search box and filters to locate specific endpoints.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>View evidence packages collected from an endpoint</p></td>
<td><p>Identify an endpoint and click <img src="./images/simulationsRightArrow=20220525102311.webp" /> to display all packages collected from an endpoint.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Archive</strong> displays the following information about evidence packages:</p>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>Package</strong>: Name of the collected evidence package</p></li>
<li><p><strong>File size</strong>: Size of the package</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Collection</strong>: Collection status of the evidence</p>
<p>Collection statuses include:</p>
<ul>
<li><p><em>In progress...</em> (<img src="./images/inProgress=763d5319-3680-4b10-abca-a75782d8eb48.webp" />): <strong>Forensics</strong>is processing the evidence.</p></li>
<li><p><em>Successful</em> (<img src="./images/successful=d385863c-ae1f-4a76-8500-6d60d7a2de8f.webp" />): <strong>Forensics</strong>processed the evidence.</p></li>
<li><p><em>Partially Successful</em> (<img src="./images/partSuccesssful=cc794327-0b7f-45d7-ac71-463e5d02d6d1.webp" />): <strong>Forensics</strong> could not process some of the evidence types in the package.</p></li>
<li><p><em>Unsuccessful</em> (<img src="./images/unsuccessful=e4c3ece6-3776-45dc-b99a-67195d7d21e0.webp" />): An error or time-out occurred when processing the evidence package.</p></li>
</ul></li>
<li><p><strong>Source</strong>: The product or method that uploaded the evidence package to <strong>Forensics</strong></p></li>
<li><p><strong>Collected</strong>: The date and time <strong>Forensics</strong> received the uploaded evidence package</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Deletion</strong>: The date and time that <strong>Forensics</strong> will delete the package</p>


:::danger[Warning]

<p><strong>Forensics</strong> automatically deletes evidence packages one year after upload.</p>


:::

</li>
</ul></td>
