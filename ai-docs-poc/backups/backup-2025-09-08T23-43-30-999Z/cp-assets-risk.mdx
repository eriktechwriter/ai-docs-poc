::: {role="main"}
The Assets at Risk widget offers a high-level summary of at-risk cloud assets within your organization, organized by asset type. From this widget, you can:

- **Expand your view**: Click the **View more** link to access the Attack Surface Discovery page.
- **Asset type details**: Click the link for any asset type to see a detailed list of at-risk assets in the Attack Surface Discovery page.
:::
