::: {role="main"}
Start a free trial of select Trend Vision One features to experience the benefits before committing to allocating credits or using pay-as-you-go.

Refer to the following list of Trend Vision One features offering trials, and follow the relevant instructions to start your free trial:

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 20%" />
<col style="width: 20%" />
<col style="width: 60%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><p>Trial</p></th>
<th><p>Duration</p></th>
<th><p>Activation instructions</p></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Agentic SIEM - Third-party log collection</strong></p></td>
<td><p>60 days</p></td>
<td><ol>
<li><p>Go to <strong>Data Source and Log Management → Data sources and retention → Third-party log repositories</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Start free trial</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Confirm the details of the trial and click <strong>Activate</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Once the trial starts, click <strong>Get started</strong>.</p>


:::note

<p>Your data usage for Agentic SIEM - Third-party log collection does not count towards your credit allocations or pay-as-you-go billing until the trial ends. However, analytic and archival retention of Trend Vision One data is not within the trial scope and requires credits.</p>


:::

</li>
<li><p>Within 10 days of the trial's expiration date, you can go to the <strong>Credit settings</strong> drawer and click <strong>Extend your trial by 30 days</strong>. You can only extend the trial once.</p></li>
<li><p>To stop using this feature after the trial ends, click the <strong>disable auto-allocation</strong> link in the <strong>Credit settings</strong> drawer, and click <strong>Turn off automatic allocation</strong>.</p>


:::warning[Important]

<p>If you disable automatic allocation, after the trial ends, any log collectors or log repositories you created are removed, and your data is only retained for the free retention period. To prevent this disruption of service, return to the <strong>Credit settings</strong> drawer and click <strong>Enable automatic allocation</strong>.</p>


:::

</li>
</ol></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Cyber Risk Exposure Management</strong></p></td>
<td><p>30 days</p></td>
<td><ol>
<li><p>Go to any <strong>Cyber Risk Exposure Management</strong> app and click <strong>Start Free Trial</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>In the confirmation window, click <strong>Start Free Trial</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Go to the <strong>Credits &amp; Pay-As-You-Go Settings</strong> drawer and use the toggles to enable credit auto-allocation for assessed desktops and servers and for cloud accounts.</p></li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Submit</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>To stop using Cyber Risk Exposure Management after the trial ends, return to the drawer and disable credit allocation for assessed desktops and servers or cloud accounts.</p>


:::warning[Important]

<p>If you disable credit allocation for Cyber Risk Exposure Management, after the trial ends, your desktops, servers, and cloud accounts are no longer assessed. To prevent this disruption of service, enable credit allocation for both features.</p>


:::

</li>
</ol></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Virtual Network Sensor</strong></p></td>
<td><p>30 days</p></td>
<td><ol>
<li><p>Go to <strong>Network Inventory → Virtual Network Sensor</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Deploy Virtual Network Sensor</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>In the window that appears, enable or disable auto-allocation and click <strong>Get Started</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Start Trial Now</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>To stop using this feature after the trial ends, you can go to the <strong>Credit Usage</strong> drawer, use the toggle to disable credit auto-allocation, and click <strong>Save</strong>.</p>


:::warning[Important]

<p>If you disable automatic allocation, after the trial ends, your Virtual Network Sensors no longer send detection data to Trend Vision One. To prevent this disruption of service, enable the <strong>Auto-allocate credits when trial period ends</strong> setting.</p>


:::

</li>
</ol>
<p>For more information on allocating credits to Virtual Network Sensor, see <a href="trend-vision-one-credit-network-inventory">Credit allocation for Network Security</a>.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>XDR for Cloud</strong></p></td>
<td><p>60 days</p></td>
<td><ol>
<li><p>Go to <strong>Cloud Accounts → Credit settings</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Select <strong>Start your free trial of XDR for Cloud</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Get started</strong>.</p>


:::note

<p>The <strong>Credit settings</strong> usage graph displays your trial period data usage for XDR for Cloud, but this usage does not count towards your credit allocations or pay-as-you-go billing until the trial ends.</p>


:::

</li>
<li><p>Within 10 days of the trial's expiration date, you can return to the <strong>Credit settings</strong> drawer and click <strong>Extend your trial by 30 days</strong>. You can only extend the trial once.</p></li>
<li><p>At any time, click <strong>Skip the trial and allocate credits</strong> to start using XDR for Cloud as a paid feature.</p></li>
</ol>
<p>For more information on allocating credits to XDR for Cloud, see <a href="trend-vision-one-xdr-for-cloud-usage">Estimating and monitoring XDR for Cloud usage</a></p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Zero Trust Secure Access</strong></p></td>
<td><p>60 days</p></td>
<td><ol>
<li><p>Go to <strong>Zero Trust Secure Access → Secure Access Overview</strong>.</p></li>
<li><p>Go to the <strong>Credit settings</strong> drawer to begin the free trial.</p></li>
<li><p>Choose either <strong>Automatically allocate credits after the trial ends</strong> or <strong>Decide later</strong>.</p>


:::warning[Important]

<p>If you choose <strong>Decide later</strong>, after the trial ends, you must allocate credits to regain access to <strong>Zero Trust Secure Access</strong> features. To prevent this disruption of service, enable automatic credit allocation.</p>


:::

</li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Apply</strong>.</p></li>
</ol>
<p>For more information, see <a href="trend-vision-one-ztsa-credit-settings">Zero Trust Secure Access credit settings</a>.</p></td>
</tr>
