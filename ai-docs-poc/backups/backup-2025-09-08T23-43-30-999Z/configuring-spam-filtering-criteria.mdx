:::: {role="main"}
The **Spam**, **Phishing**, **Graymail**, **Web Reputation**, **Social engineering attack**, or **Unusual signal** criteria allow you to create policy rules that take actions on these types of potentially unwanted messages.

:::note

Cloud Email Gateway Protection does not apply content-based heuristic spam, BEC, phishing, graymail, Web reputation, social engineering attack, or unusual signal rules to email messages received from email addresses and domains listed on the **Approved Senders** screen.
:::
