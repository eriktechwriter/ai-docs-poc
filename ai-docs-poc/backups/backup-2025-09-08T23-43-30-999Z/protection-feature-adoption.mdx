<td><p>Feature Adoption Status</p></td>
<td><p>Shows whether you have adopted sufficient features for protection against various threats.</p>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>Good</strong>: Feature adoption is sufficient. All features with high (<img src="./images/adoption_status_good=GUID-467933A9-7097-4D01-9D79-3FA0B71CFD18=en-us=.webp" />) and medium (<img src="./images/adoption_status_partial=GUID-E0AC1D18-BD21-4AC3-9D88-40146372BB10=en-us=.webp" />) importance are adopted.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Partial</strong>: Feature adoption is partial. All features with high importance (<img src="./images/adoption_status_good=GUID-467933A9-7097-4D01-9D79-3FA0B71CFD18=en-us=.webp" />) are adopted but at least one feature with medium importance (<img src="./images/adoption_status_partial=GUID-E0AC1D18-BD21-4AC3-9D88-40146372BB10=en-us=.webp" />) is not adopted.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Warning</strong>: Feature adoption is insufficient. At least one feature with high importance (<img src="./images/adoption_status_good=GUID-467933A9-7097-4D01-9D79-3FA0B71CFD18=en-us=.webp" />) is not adopted.</p></li>
</ul>


:::note

<p>If a feature is enabled in at least one policy for a service, Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection regards the feature as adopted for the service.</p>


:::

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>"Refresh" button</p></td>
<td><p>Allows you to manually check the latest configuration status after you adopt recommended features.</p>


:::note

<p>Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection performs a daily scheduled check instead of updating the status in real time.</p>


:::

</td>
</tr>

<td><p>Details about feature adoption</p></td>
<td><p>Shows the status of feature adoption for protection against each type of threats, including BEC, phishing, ransomware, malicious URL, malicious file, and spam.</p>
<p>Click a threat type and go to the policies to enable the recommended features.</p></td>
