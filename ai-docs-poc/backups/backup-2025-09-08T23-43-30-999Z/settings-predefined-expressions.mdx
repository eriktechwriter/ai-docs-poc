:::: {role="main"}
:::note

Predefined expressions cannot be modified or deleted.
:::

### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  Go to **Policies → Policy Resources → DLP Data Identifiers**.

2.  Click the **Expression** tab.

3.  Click the expression name.

4.  View settings in the screen that opens.
