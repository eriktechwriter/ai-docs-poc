</ul></td>

<li><p><strong>Potentially unwanted app</strong>: classified as grayware and a user may perceive as unwanted.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Privacy-leaking app</strong>: leaks personal private data such as phone information, GPS location, and Wi-Fi data.</p></li>
</ul></td>
