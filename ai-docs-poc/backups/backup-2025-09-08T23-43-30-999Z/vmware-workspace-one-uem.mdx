Perform the integration to discover VMware Workspace ONE UEM-enrolled mobile devices and deploy the Mobile Security for Business to the devices for increased visibility and management.

With VMware Workspace ONE UEM integration, you can easily deploy the Mobile Security for Business app as well as app configuration to managed mobile devices in your organization with zero touch. **Mobile Security** can then leverage the app configuration to enroll the mobile devices with it.
