Trend Vision One™ – Cloud Risk Management offers multiple reports of rules run and check success and failures. These include

- [All Checks Report](all-checks-report.mdx) - current state of your Account against all rules enabled and active on your account.
- [Configured Reports](configured-reports.mdx) - custom reports using [filters](filter-search.mdx) that can be saved and scheduled for recurrent generation.
- [Cloud Risk Management Report](cloud-posture-report.mdx) - all rules run against each account daily.
- [Compliance and Cloud Risk Management Reports](compliance-reports.mdx) - report that check compliance with a specific [Compliance Standard or Framework](https://www.cloudconformity.com/conformity-rules/Compliance/).

Checks can be exported either by:

- [Generate and Download Report](generate-download-report.mdx), or
- Cloud Risk Management [API for Checks](https://automation.trendmicro.com/cloudone/conformity/)
