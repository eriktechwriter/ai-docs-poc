Review and set policies for managed email domains in the Email Asset Inventory app.

When accessing the Email Asset Inventory app, you can click on any of the items in the **Email Domain Inventory** section of the left panel to display a list of associated email domains managed by your protection solution. From this view, you can perform several actions.

</tr>
</thead>

<tr>
<td><p>View email domain details</p></td>
<td><p>Click on an email domain to open the <strong>Detailed Profile</strong> screen</p>
<p>The <strong>Detailed Profile</strong> screen consists of two tabs.</p>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>Basic</strong>: Provides basic information about the email domain such as the inbound/outbound configuration and policy status.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Policy</strong>: Provides an overview of enabled policies. Click <strong>Manage Policy</strong> to configure your inbound or outbound policy settings.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
