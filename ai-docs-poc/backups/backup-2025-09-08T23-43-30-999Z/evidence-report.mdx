Search, filter, and organize all the digital evidence collected from endpoints.

The following table outlines the actions available in the **Evidence Report** screen.

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 20%" />
<col style="width: 80%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><p>Action</p></th>
<th><p>Description</p></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><p>Filter evidence</p></td>
<td><p>Locate key pieces of evidence by using the <a href="trend-vision-one-windows-evidence-types">evidence categories</a>, search field, and the <strong>Package</strong> menu.</p>
<p>The <strong>Package</strong> list only displays evidence packages collected from the endpoint added to the workspace.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>View high-risk elements</p></td>
<td><p>The <strong>Highlights</strong> section of the evidence report displays all the high-risk pieces of evidence found in the collected evidence. Forensics uses the Trend Micro threat intelligence service to classify elements as high-risk.</p>
<p>You can use the <strong>Highlights</strong> section as a starting point for your investigations.</p>
<p>The information in the <strong>Highlights</strong> section does not update automatically. To update the information, you must collect evidence from your endpoints again.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Add evidence to timeline</p></td>
<td><p>Add key evidence to your workspace timeline to gain insights into the context of an incident. You can add up to 1,000 elements to the timeline at a time.</p>
<p>Select one or more pieces of evidence, click <strong>Add Selected to Timeline</strong>, select a timestamp type, and click <strong>Create</strong>.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>View execution context</p></td>
<td><p>The <strong>Execution Context</strong> panel lets you to see all the events that happened before and after the execution of a program.</p>
<p>Right-click a piece of evidence, then click <strong>View Execution Context</strong> to see the execution context of a specific element.</p>
<p>The Execution Context panel can display up to 20,000 elements.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>View related threat intelligence from VirusTotal</p></td>
<td><p>Threat intelligence from VirusTotal facilitates thorough investigation of possible threats in your environment.</p>
<p>Right-click URLs, domains, IPs, or file SHA-1 and select <strong>VirusTotal</strong> to check the related element information from VirusTotal.</p></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
