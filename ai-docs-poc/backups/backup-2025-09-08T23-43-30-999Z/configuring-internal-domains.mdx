### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  In Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection, go to **Policies → Global Settings → User-Defined Lists → Internal Domain List**.

2.  Add an internal domain.

    1.  Specify a domain name that you want to exclude from scanning.

    2.  Click **Add** to add the domain to the internal domain list.

3.  Click **Click here** to add the existing domains in your user information to the internal domain list.

4.  Click **Import** to import an internal domain list.

5.  Click **Export** to export the existing internal domain list.

6.  Click **Save**.
:::
