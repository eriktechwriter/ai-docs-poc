<td><p><strong>Contact Information</strong></p></td>
<td><p>Add contacts to receive alert and report notifications.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>You must add at least one contact during the initial setup. The Managed Services team may contact the phone number you specified in an emergency.</p></li>
<li><p>To change your preferences in receiving phone call notifications for different alert severities, contact the Managed Services team.</p></li>
<li><p>By default, the first contact you add is the primary contact. If there is only one contact, the only contact must allow both alert and report notifications.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>Manage existing contacts that receive alert and report notifications.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>The email address is mandatory for the primary contact, and for contacts that receive report notifications only.</p></li>
<li><p>Update contact information or remove contacts if necessary. To change the primary contact, move another contact to the top of the list.</p></li>
<li><p>Each contact must have a unique email address.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Response Approval</strong></p></td>
<td><p>Configure response approval settings to specify how response actions proceed before execution.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>For more information about actions the operations team can perform on your behalf, see <a href="trend-vision-one-response-actions-mdr">Response actions</a>.</p></li>
<li><p>For more information about enabling request auto approval, see <a href="trend-vision-one-config-response-approval-settings">Configuring Response Approval Settings</a>.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Asset Management</strong></p></td>
<td><p>Upload an asset list as a CSV file to provide a centralized view of your critical assets to the Managed Services operations team.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Click <strong>Import Assets</strong> to upload your asset list. The CSV file must be smaller than 256 KB.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Cross-App Management</strong></p></td>
<td><p>Automatically update the status of related Workbench alerts when the MDR case status changes.</p>
<p>Automatically create a ServiceNow ticket when an MDR case is created.</p></td>
</tr>
