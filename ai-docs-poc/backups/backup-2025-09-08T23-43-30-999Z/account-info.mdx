::: {role="main"}
Configure basic settings for your connected AWS accounts.

The Account Information tab in the Cloud Account Settings screen allows you to view and configure basic account settings. After changing a field, click **Save Changes**. Updating information on the Account Information tab does not require performing a stack update.
