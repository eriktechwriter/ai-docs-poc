:::: {role="main"}
Organize data and Trend Vision One app assets based on your organizational structure to apply for different visibility and management purposes.

The **Asset Visibility Management** app allows you to group your organization's data and Trend Vision One app assets in a centralized location. For more information, see [What is Asset Visibility Management](what-is-asset-visibility-management.mdx).

:::warning[Important]

Only customers that have updated to the [Foundation Services release](update-foundation-services-release.mdx) have access to the feature.
:::
