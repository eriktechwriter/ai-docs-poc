View details about attack-related risk events to better understand possible impacts and available remediation actions.

To change the status of risk events, click **Manage risk event** to go to the **Threat and Exposure Management** app.

The following table details the tabs in the attack risk event profile screen.

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 25%" />
<col style="width: 75%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><p>Tab</p></th>
<th><p>Description</p></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><p>Basic</p></td>
<td><p>Displays general information about the risk event, as well as mitigation options, preventative measures, and informational references.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Impacted assets</p></td>
<td><p>Lists the assets in your organization impacted by the risk event, as well as the latest risk score, account type, job title, data source, and date of detection.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Related alerts</p></td>
<td><p>Lists all <strong>Workbench</strong> alerts related to the risk event, as well as the case management status, alert score, associated incident, and time of creation.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Related exposures</p></td>
<td><p>Lists all exposure risk events related to the attack detections.</p></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
