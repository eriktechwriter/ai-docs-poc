Trend Vision One™ – Cloud Risk Management organizes all [rules](introduction-rules.mdx) into 5 categories to identify distinct outcomes targeted by public cloud infrastructure users. These categories are based on the [AWS Well-Architected Framework](https://aws.amazon.com/architecture/well-architected/) but follow Cloud Risk Management's rule classification framework.

*Categories:*

### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  Security

2.  Cost Optimisation

3.  Operational Excellence

4.  Reliability

5.  Performance Efficiency

6.  Sustainability

### Next steps {#next-steps}

A rule can belong to 1 or more categories.
