::::: {role="main"}
Use information in Threat and Exposure Management to quickly assess and mitigate vulnerabilities related to users, devices, and other assets in your organization.

:::warning[Important]

You must allocate credits to Cyber Risk Exposure Management to use **Threat and Exposure Management**. For more information, see [Credit requirements for Trend Vision One solutions, capabilities, and features](credit-req-for-apps-services.mdx).
:::

:::note

**Threat and Exposure Management** provides statistics based off data from the last 30 days.

The data available for a user to view and drill down depends on the asset visibility scope of the current user. For more information about the asset visibility scope, see [Asset Visibility Management](asset-visibility-management.mdx).
:::
