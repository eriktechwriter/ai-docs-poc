View information about the **XDR detection** risk factor, which is determined by Workbench alerts.

**Threat and Exposure Management** uses data from your organization's Workbench alerts to determine the risk levels of your users and devices. The XDR detection risk factor contributes to your [attack level](attack-overview.mdx).
