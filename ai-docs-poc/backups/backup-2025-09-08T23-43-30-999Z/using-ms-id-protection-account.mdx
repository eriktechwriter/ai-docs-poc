::: {role="main"}
A Microsoft Identity Protection account grants Cloud Email and Collaboration Protection the permission to obtain high risk event data about your Microsoft 365 users from Microsoft Identity Protection. As one source of the Internal User Risk Analytics widgets, the data help you gain insights into the top risks and risk trends regarding the Microsoft 365 users in your organization.
