May 2, 2025—New Created by and Closed by fields and filters in Case Management allow you to view cases based on whether they were opened or closed by a security playbook, Trend Vision One user, or third-party ticketing system. With these fields, you can easily assess the effectiveness of automation and analyst performance for reporting accuracy and informed operational decisions.
