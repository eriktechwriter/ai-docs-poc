::: {role="main"}
Configure basic settings for your connected Alibaba Cloud accounts.

Access the Account Information tab in the Account Settings screen to view and configure basic settings. After changing a field, click **Save Changes**. Updating information on the Account Information tab does not require performing a resource update.
