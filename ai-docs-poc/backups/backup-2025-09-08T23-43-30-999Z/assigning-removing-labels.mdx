### Procedure {#procedure}

1.  Go to **Directories → Users/Endpoints**.

2.  Select the **Endpoints** to view, or search for specific endpoints.

3.  To associate a custom label with an endpoint:

    - Click the endpoint row and click **Tasks → Assign/Remove Labels**.

    - Right-click the endpoint row and click **Assign/Remove Labels**.

4.  In the **Assign/Remove Labels** dialog, select or clear the necessary label(s) from the list, and click **Save**.

    You can verify the proper association of labels with the selected endpoints by selecting a label from the **Labels** list, and checking that the selected endpoint displays properly.
:::
